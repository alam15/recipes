Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
 - 2/3 cup cocoa
 - 3 cups powdered sugar
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and stir in cocoa.
  2. Alternately add powdered sugar and milk. Beat until light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in vanilla.  If necessary, add a small amount of additional milk.
  4. If frosting becomes too thin, beat in small amount of powdered sugar.
	 Frosts 13x9-inch cake generally, or fills and frosts 8- or 9-inch two-layer cake.
